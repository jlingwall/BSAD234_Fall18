Homework 09

Working with groups is encouraged. Designate someone to turn in a single copy, with all group members' names, on Blackboard.

Analyze the following situations in terms of the First Amendment. When we provided a specific test in class (such as for content-neutral speech), be sure to walk through the elements rather than talking in generalities.

  1. Truman imposes a rule which prohibits critizing professors.
  2. Your boss at a private company says "if you criticize me, you'll lose your job."
  3. Missouri passes a law which prohibits labeling lab-grown meat as "meat".
  4. Missouri passes a law stating that all news articles must be pre-approved by a state agency before being published within the state.
  5. A parent sues a school district because a biology textbook contains material the parent finds religiously objectionable as to the creation of life on earth, claiming using a different textbook is accommodation for his religion.
  6. Suppose you wish to claim religious accommodation at work, to not be scheduled on a particular day of the week. Give an example of when this might be accommodated, and when it might not.
