Homework 05

Working with groups is encouraged. Designate someone to turn in a single copy, with all group members' names, on Blackboard.

  1. In your own words, what is Kant’s “categorical imperative”?
  2. Give an example of how someone might apply the categorical imperative in practice.
  3. How does someone who follows utilitarianism make ethical decisions?
  4. How does someone who follows a “principles of rights” ethical framework make decisions?
  5. Using the Say Yes to the Dress case discussed in class, show how someone using the four ethical frameworks from Chapter 5 § 3 would evaluate the situation. For example, would the categorical imperative suggest suing about this? Did the show violate a principle of rights?
