Homework 10

Working with groups is encouraged. Designate someone to turn in a single copy, with all group members' names, on Blackboard.

This homework finishes up Constitutional Law by applying the 14th Amendment in three Equal Protection situations:

  1. Suppose Kirksville passes an ordinance limited food trucks to those with existing licenses, so getting a new food truck in town is impossible. Does this violate the Equal Protection Clause? (By treating new versus old food vendors differently?)
  2. Suppose Missouri passed a law segregating public schools on the basis of religion. Does this violate the Equal Protection Clause?
  3. Suppose Truman decided to limit enrollment to men only. Does this violate the Equal Protection Clause?
