Homework 01

Working with groups is encouraged. Designate someone to turn in a single copy, with all group members' names, on Blackboard.

  1. Find the text of Roe v. Wade. Where did you find it? What does its citation mean? Does stare decisis mean Roe will always be the law? How does Roe build off of a case called Griswold v. Connecticut?
  2. Work through problem 1-1 under "Business Scenarios".
  3. Work through problem 1-2 under "Business Scenarios".
  4. Using bullet points, sketch out IRAC for the Ross case on page 21.In other words, in one or two sentences each, state the Issue, the Rule, how the court Analyzed the Rule, and the Conclusion.
  5. Work through the "Legal Reasoning Activity" 1-7. Answer each of the questions.
